FAQ

Questions we
get asked a lot

Is D-ONE AI a replacement for therapy? +
No. D-ONE AI is a supportive companion, not a licensed therapist. We encourage professional help for serious mental health concerns. Think of us as a safe first step or daily check-in.
Are my conversations stored? +
No. We don't store, log, or analyze your conversations. Each session is private and ephemeral — once you close the chat, it's gone.
What if I'm in a crisis? +
D-ONE AI is trained to recognize crisis signals and will immediately prioritize your safety by providing emergency resources and encouraging you to reach out to professionals or trusted people in your life.
Is D-ONE AI free to use? +
Yes, completely free. We believe mental health support should have no financial barrier. D-ONE AI is accessible to everyone.
How is D-ONE different from other chatbots? +
D-ONE is purpose-built for mental wellness — not a general assistant. Every response is tuned for empathy, safety, and emotional support rather than productivity or information retrieval.
Can I use D-ONE in my language? +
Yes! D-ONE AI can understand and respond in multiple languages. Just start typing in your preferred language and it will follow your lead.
